# Resume Optimization Example

## Task

Improve a resume for general job-readiness without a target job description.

## Inputs

- original resume
- target language

## Workflow

1. confirm the original resume is the source of truth
2. identify the requested language
3. rewrite for clarity, strength, and compactness
4. review names, dates, and titles

## Expected Output

- stronger bullets
- clearer sectioning
- no fake facts
- application-ready tone
